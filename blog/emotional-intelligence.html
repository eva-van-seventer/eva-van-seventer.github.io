Emotional Intelligence

What is EQ?

Emotional intelligence is basically the ability to intelligently handle your emotions. It consists of four domains.

Self-management- handling your distressing emotions in an effective way. It involves controlling impulsive feelings and behaviors, taking initiative, following through on commitments, and adapting to changing circumstances.

Social Awareness -You have empathy and can understand the emotions, needs, and concerns of other people. You can pick up on emotional cues, feel comfortable socially, and recognize the power dynamics in a group or organization.

Relationship management-You know how to develop and maintain good relationships, communicate clearly, inspire and influence others, work well in a team, and manage conflict.

Difference to IQ?

IQ is how strong your ability to solve problems, use logic, and grasp and communicate complex odeas. EQ is your ability to recognize emotion in yourself and others and using that awareness to guide your decisions.

Why is it important?

To help you to able to build relationships, reduce team stress, defuse conflict and improve job satisfaction. Experts say emotional intelligence is crucial in predicting our health, happiness and success.
